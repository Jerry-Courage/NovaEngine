About OctaNova

About

Nova is a search-first opportunity engine that brings together dreamers, talents, companies and investors — helping opportunity find people and people find opportunity.

Our mission

To reduce friction in discovery and collaboration so great ideas get matched with the right people and resources — fast.

  • Search-first discovery — find the right match, not just listings.
  • Transparent profiles and verified credentials to build trust.
  • Tools for pitching, collaborating and closing deals — all in one place.

Nova Engine is built for global reach with local focus — discover opportunities in your city or across the world.

Platform Features

What Nova Engine Offers

Powerful features designed to connect ideas with capital and talent with work.

Search & Discover

Robust full-text search across profiles, pitches, roles and projects — plus advanced filters (location, role, industry, funding stage).

Smart Match Engine

AI-driven matching ranks best-fit investors, collaborators and jobs for each user and opportunity.

Pitch Hub

Create one-page pitches (with optional short video), get public feedback, and invite private investor reviews.

Collaboration Marketplace

Find co-founders, contractors, mentors and research partners — post short gigs and project calls.

Trust & Verification

Verification badges and Nova Score (reputation) help reduce risk and increase trust across the platform.

Messaging & Deals

Built-in messaging, pitch rooms and deal-tracking to take conversations from discovery to closing.
